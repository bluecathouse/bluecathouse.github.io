About the app

Daily Ab Workout FREE is a great 5 to 10 minute daily ab routine for men and women that steps you through some of the best ab-sculpting exercises you can do in the comfort of your own home. These proven workouts, demonstrated by a certified personal trainer, target all major abdominal muscles. Spending just minutes a day can strengthen your core and tone your abs. The routine's simple interface, complete with video and timer, allows you to easily follow along and understand each exercise.

FEATURES:
• Two different 5 to 10 minute ab-sculpting workouts
• Great for both men and women
• Video showing how to do each exercise
• Developed by a certified personal trainer
• On-screen instructions and timer
• No internet required to do the workouts

If you like Daily Ab Workout FREE, check out the full version which features:
